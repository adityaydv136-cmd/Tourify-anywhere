Manali

🌍 About Manali

Manali is India's adventure capital — where the Beas River roars through valleys framed by snow-capped peaks rising above 4,000 meters. It's the gateway to Rohtang Pass, Lahaul-Spiti, and Ladakh's breathtaking landscapes.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Solang Valley
  • Rohtang Pass
  • Hadimba Devi Temple
  • Old Manali
  • Beas Kund Trek

🖼️ Photo Gallery

🍽️ Famous Food

Trout Fish curry, Tibetan Momos, Thukpa noodle soup, Siddu with ghee, and local apple wine from Kullu valley.

🎯 Popular Activities

River rafting on the Beas, skiing at Solang Valley, paragliding, snowmobiling, zorbing, and trekking to ancient glaciers.

🏨 Recommended Hotels

  • Snow Peak Retreat
  • The Himalayan Resort
  • Hotel Mountain Face
  • Manali Heights

💡 Why Visit Manali?

Manali is the ultimate escape for adventure lovers and honeymooners alike — dramatic scenery, adrenaline-pumping activities, and mountain tranquility all in one valley.
