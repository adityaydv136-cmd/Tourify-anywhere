Agra

🌍 About Agra

Agra is one of India's most iconic cities, home to Mughal architecture that has stood for centuries. The city breathes history through every stone — from the majestic Taj Mahal at sunrise to the towering walls of Agra Fort.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Taj Mahal
  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehpur Sikri
  • Mehtab Bagh
  • Itmad-ud-Daulah

🖼️ Photo Gallery

🍽️ Famous Food

Agra Petha, Bedai & Jalebi, Mughlai Biryani, and the world-famous Dalmoth snack are culinary highlights of this city.

🎯 Popular Activities

Sunrise photography at Taj Mahal, heritage walks through Agra Fort, shopping for marble inlay handicrafts, camel rides, and moonlight viewing at Mehtab Bagh.

🏨 Recommended Hotels

  • Hotel Taj Resorts
  • Radisson Hotel Agra
  • Hotel Amar
  • Crystal Sarovar Premiere

💡 Why Visit Agra?

Agra offers an irreplaceable encounter with one of humanity's greatest architectural achievements. The Taj Mahal at sunrise is a once-in-a-lifetime moment no photograph fully captures.
