Delhi

🌍 About Delhi

Delhi is India's capital and its most complex, layered city — where the ancient Mughal empire's heart still beats at Red Fort and Jama Masjid, while the modern world roars through its metro, malls, and startup hubs. Every colony is a different century.

📍 Top Attractions

  • India Gate
  • Red Fort
  • Qutub Minar
  • Lotus Temple
  • Akshardham Temple

🖼️ Photo Gallery

🍽️ Famous Food

Chole Bhature, Butter Chicken from its birthplace, street Chaat at Chandni Chowk, Paranthe Wali Gali specialties, and the iconic Old Delhi Nihari.

🎯 Popular Activities

Chandni Chowk food walks, Old Delhi rickshaw tours, Red Fort light-and-sound show, Hauz Khas nightlife, Qutub Minar sunset photography, and shopping at Janpath market.

🏨 Recommended Hotels

  • The Taj Palace
  • ITC Maurya
  • Radisson Blu Delhi
  • The Lalit New Delhi

💡 Why Visit Delhi?

Delhi is where every story of India converges. Conquerors, poets, saints, and rebels all left their mark here. Walking these streets is walking through 3,000 years of history.
