Kerala

🌍 About Kerala

Kerala is India's emerald treasure — where backwater canals stretch endlessly through coconut groves, ancient Ayurveda healing traditions thrive, and the Western Ghats rise into mist. Called God's Own Country for a reason that reveals itself at first sight.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Munnar Tea Hills
  • Kovalam Beach
  • Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Wayanad Forests

🖼️ Photo Gallery

🍽️ Famous Food

Appam with Stew, Kerala Sadya on banana leaf, Karimeen Pollichathu (pearl spot fish), Puttu with Kadala curry, and the iconic Kerala Prawn Curry.

🎯 Popular Activities

Traditional houseboat cruises on Vembanad Lake, Ayurveda spa treatments, kathakali dance performances, wildlife safaris in Periyar, and sunrise treks in Munnar.

🏨 Recommended Hotels

  • Kumarakom Lake Resort
  • Taj Bekal Resort
  • The Leela Kovalam
  • Spice Village Resort

💡 Why Visit Kerala?

Kerala offers peace, beauty, and wellness in a single destination. The backwater sunsets from a houseboat deck are among the most magical experiences India offers.
