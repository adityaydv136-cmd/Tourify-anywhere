Shimla

🌍 About Shimla

Shimla, the Queen of Hills, sits at 2,206 meters above sea level — a colonial-era hill station wrapped in cedar forests, draped in snowfall and alive with the charm of a bygone era. The Mall Road and Ridge remain India's most scenic promenades.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Mall Road
  • Kufri
  • Jakhoo Temple
  • The Ridge
  • Viceregal Lodge

🖼️ Photo Gallery

🍽️ Famous Food

Himachali Siddu, Madra (chickpea curry), Chha Gosht, Aktori pancakes, and locally brewed apple cider.

🎯 Popular Activities

Trekking through cedar forests, snowfall photography, ice skating at Kufri, shopping on Mall Road, and heritage walks past British-era buildings.

🏨 Recommended Hotels

  • Hotel Willow Banks
  • Snow Valley Resort
  • Radisson Shimla
  • Hotel Combermere

💡 Why Visit Shimla?

Shimla is perfect for those seeking cool mountain air, colonial charm and the magic of snowfall. It's India's most beloved winter hill station for a reason.
