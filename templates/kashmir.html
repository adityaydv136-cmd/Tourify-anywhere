Kashmir

🌍 About Kashmir

Kashmir — Paradise on Earth. Shimmering Dal Lake with its floating gardens, the meadows of Gulmarg blanketed in snow, the saffron fields of Pampore glowing gold. Kashmir is not just a place; it's a feeling of pure, overwhelming beauty.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Dal Lake & Shikara Rides
  • Gulmarg Gondola
  • Pahalgam Valley
  • Sonmarg Glacier
  • Mughal Gardens

🖼️ Photo Gallery

🍽️ Famous Food

Rogan Josh, Kashmiri Pulao, Dum Aloo, Gushtaba meatballs, and the warming Kahwa saffron tea — a cuisine worthy of Mughal emperors.

🎯 Popular Activities

Shikara rides at sunrise on Dal Lake, skiing and gondola rides at Gulmarg, pony treks to Betaab Valley, carpet weaving workshops, and overnight houseboat stays.

🏨 Recommended Hotels

  • The Khyber Himalayan Resort
  • Vivanta Dal View
  • Hotel Pine Spring
  • Radisson Srinagar

💡 Why Visit Kashmir?

Kashmir transcends description. It must be experienced — the snow-capped peaks reflected in still lake water, the call to prayer echoing through cedar forests at dawn.
